Privacy Policy

At the moment Blitz does not collect any user data.





Blitz is an app developed by myself using react native to help students at my university to connect to one another. The app automates the creation of groupchats for all courses at my university each semester.


To do this, I have webscraped the full list of courses on offer at bu and update the list each semester.

The app contains user generated communities that function similar to facebook groups and reddit communities.

Users can make posts that other Users can repsond to. Such as the BU dorm swap community.

Users can make posts that other Users can repsond to.

Messages from groupchats, classes, and dms can all be viewed by users in one place

Users have access to a directory of all other users.

They can create private groupchats with their friends.

The app has a market section for people that want to be able to sell things.

Individuals that have been approved will have the ability to post listings in the market section.

Right now, the app can't process transactions so item listers need to be contacted by buyers.

Finally, users can update their profiles to better describe them.
