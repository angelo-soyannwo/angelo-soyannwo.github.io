What Do You Want Out of Life and Who Do You Want to Be?

Begin with the end in mind. If one wishes to get what one wants out of life in my opinion, one should think about the best possible set of actions to get to their goals, and orient their life towards the goal. I think that one should maximize for what they want to achieve in life. This involves deep internal reflection in order to figure out what that means for oneself. What values are you going to uphold, what is the overall ethos of your life.

For me my goal in life is to attain and use strength for the sake of those who lack strength of their own. I believe that moral, physical, and mental virtue should be sought in order manifest God's order in the world. This entails perfecting oneself to the greatest possible extent and working to create a community that helps and enables others to do the same. I believe that I should aim to be perfect and strive towards forging a character that can truly be described as bearing God's image.
While this is difficult it forces me to actively become better and develop other areas of my life such that I can stil compete for the things that I want even when christian morality puts me at a short term handicap.
For example, always trying to be honest forces you to confront the truth. In the short term this means that you may not get what you want but, loving the truth forces you to make better decisions because you cannot lie.
